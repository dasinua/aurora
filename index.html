About company

Millions of people dream of their own home. It is important for us to give people the opportunity to fulfill their dreams!

For 4 years we have been holding unique regional events, acting as organizers of specialized events in real estate.

We create a space for communication between developers, real estate agencies, banks, businesses and potential customers.

Our company provides various formats of events: seminars, round tables, presentations, fairs, exhibitions, training programs, live radio and TV.

During the events, all its participants receive invaluable experience and knowledge, new contacts and contacts of potential partners and customers.

Cooperating with the best developers in the top 10 of their regions, leading real estate agencies, major banks and city-forming enterprises, we have already conducted more than 150 events in 17 regions of Russia.

By the end of 2018, our partner network has more than 300 real estate agencies, 55 development companies, 21 industrial enterprises and 7 top banks in the country.                      As part of our events, it is important for us to create a space of trust and professionalism, where new, bright acquaintances develop into reliable, long-term cooperation!
